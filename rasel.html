Frequently Asked Questions

How is Twos different than notes? +
With Twos, you are capturing things (individual pieces of information) which are faster, more flexible, and easier to organize.

Your things in Twos can be notes, to-dos, reminders, ideas, events & more.With notes, you are writing static text.

Text cannot be easily converted into different types of things which leads to using many different apps.
Is Twos free? +
Yes, Twos is free to use on mobile, web, and desktop.

Some features, like tags and highlight colors, cost $2 or 200 coins one-time (mobile app only).

All features/functionality are free to use on web and desktop apps.

By subscribing to Twos Plus, you get all existing and future upgrades on mobile.
How safe is Twos? +
Everything you create in Twos is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Data is stored in secure, encrypted servers by well-known, trusted companies and protected with token authentication.
What are PALs? +
PALs stand for 'Personal Active Lists' which are Twos' AI features that improve the user experience.

Currently, PALs cover 31+ AI 'smart suggestions' that generate links and functionality by simply writing things down.

For example, when you write movie titles, PALs will generate links to IMDB and streaming services.

Simply write things down in Twos and see how PALs can help you get things done.
What integrations are currently available? +
Twos currently integrates with all calendar providers (Google, Apple, Outlook, etc.). Twos also integrates with texts, emails, WhatsApp, and Siri to quickly add things to your daily list.

Visit side menu > settings > integrations to see and set up your Twos integrations.

Twos PALs (via smart suggestions) currently integrates with Amazon, Walmart, Google Maps, Apple Music, Doordash, and many more.

You can see these integrations by visiting side menu > settings > PALs
